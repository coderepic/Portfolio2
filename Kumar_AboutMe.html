About Me

Archit Kumar

kumar5963@mydusd.org
Dublin, California

Hello! My name is Archit Kumar. I am currently a junior in Dublin High School, and an aspiring software and video game designer.

Throughout recent years, video games have emerged not only as a form of entertainment, but as an invaluable tool to numerous professionals, including doctors, city planners, vehicle designers, and architects. As a game designer, I plan to use my artistic and technological skills to create tools to aid in all aspects of societal growth and innovation.

My career in video game design began when I was seven years old, when I first got my hands on the Scratch programming tool. I was fascinated by my ability to bring my own worlds and characters to life using simple programming. Since then, I have remained dedicated to mastering the art of game design. Currently, I am working on developing my own 2D-platformer game using the Unity game engine and the C# programming language.

In addition to programming and design, I am experienced in digital animation. I completed the online community college course “Fundamentals of 3D Animation”, in which I developed the ability to manipulate and animate digital models. I have also produced and published several 2D computer animations on my YouTube channel, Toon Buffoon. My ability to digitally simulate animated behavior is a vital skill in the field of game design.

My ability as a game designer and as an animator is elevated by my fondness of graphic design. A portfolio of my artwork and comics is available on my website, Toon Landing.

In addition to my projects, my job and volunteering experience has helped me build a strong work ethic. My current role as a tutor at the Russian School of Mathematics has strengthened my fundamental skills, including teaching, speaking, and problem solving. Video game design is a highly collaborative field, so strong social skills and work ethic are vital.

Overall, my creativity, work experience, social skills, projects and work ethic have all prepared me for the challenging and rewarding career of a video game designer.
